Using iTunes

  1. Power everything on
    • Monitor
      • yellow led next to power switch means monitor is on but in standby mode
    • louder
      • blue ring around power button indicates unit is powered on
      • amber ring around power button indicates unit is powered off
    • stereo
      • no led in volume knob means unit is powered off
  2. Log in as louder
  3. Start iTunes
