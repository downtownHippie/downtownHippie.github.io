Add a new device to the WIFI Access List

  1. Click on the link for fire.zoonet.
    Provide username and password.
    Under Advanced in the left hand column, click Wireless Settings.

  2. Click the Set Up Access List button in the Wireless Card Access List section.

  3. Click the Add button.

  4. Fill out the Device Name and MAC Address text boxes, then click the Add button.

  5. Click the Apply button.

  6. On your device connect to the triangle network and provide the WIFI password.
