Put something good between your ears

Using foobar2000

  1. Turn everything on
    • the Monitor
      • yellow led next to power switch means monitor is on but in standby mode
    • louder
      • If the display is dark push the power button
    • the stereo
      • no led in volume knob means unit is powered off
      • the cd changer and amplifier turn on with the pre-amp
  3. Log in as louder
  4. foobar2000 is the alien looking icon in the taskbar
